About Sri Nisty Automotives

Your Trusted Partner in Eicher Vehicle Care Since 2009

A Decade of Excellence in Automotive Service

Established in 2009, Sri Nisty Automotives is a leading authorized service center for Eicher commercial vehicles. We are committed to providing top-notch after-sales support, combining our vast experience with a passion for excellence.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools and our highly skilled, factory-trained technicians ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible. We use only genuine Eicher spare parts to guarantee performance and reliability, minimizing downtime and keeping your business moving forward.

Our Comprehensive Services

Delivering reliable and efficient solutions to keep your Eicher vehicle in peak condition, whether in our workshop or on the road.

In-Workshop Services

General Repairs & Maintenance

Complete vehicle diagnostics and repairs for all Eicher models, including engine, transmission, and electrical systems, performed by factory-trained experts.

Periodic & Preventive Service

Scheduled maintenance plans to ensure longevity and optimal performance, helping prevent unexpected breakdowns and costly repairs.

Accident & Body Repair

State-of-the-art body shop for major and minor collision repairs, restoring your vehicle to its original condition with genuine Eicher parts.

Genuine Eicher Spares

Exclusive use of authentic Eicher spare parts to guarantee perfect fit, maximum durability, and uncompromised vehicle performance.

Eicher On-Site Support

24x7 Breakdown Assistance

Round-the-clock emergency support for breakdowns. Our mobile service vans are equipped to get you back on the road anytime, anywhere.

On-Site Repairs & Service

We bring our workshop to your location. Our experts can perform scheduled services and minor repairs right at your premises, saving you valuable time.
